What We Do

Local Moving

For over 20 years, Lange Moving Systems has been helping families in the greater Columbia area and throughout South Carolina relocate with minimal disruption to their daily lives. Whether you're moving across the state or just across town, our teams give your belongings the care and attention necessary to keep them safe on the trip.

Long-Distance/Interstate Moving

Your furniture and other belongings will face unique challenges during transport over long distances. Our movers protect your load against wide temperature and humidity fluctuations, as well as the risk of damage in a tightly packed truck over many miles of travel.

International Moving

There are many complex paperwork, customs, and legal requirements to follow in order to get your possessions transported internationally. We streamline the process of dividing your load (air, sea, stg.), utilize space in containers most efficiently, and communicate with the professionals who will unload your belongings at your destination.

Packing/Crating

Packing your furniture, antiques, artwork, and other valued items correctly is the key to getting them to their destination in good condition. Our teams use high quality, clean packing materials and expert techniques to protect every piece against scratches, bumps, moisture, stress, and other factors that could damage them along the way. If you choose to do the packing yourself, we offer free recycled material and self packing tips.
